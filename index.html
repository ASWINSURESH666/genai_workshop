Workshop on Generative AI and LLM's

The Department of Computer Science at Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT) is organizing a five-day workshop on Generative AI and LLM from July 29 to August 2, 2024. This workshop is designed for faculty members, research scholars, postgraduate students, and industry professionals with a foundational understanding of Deep Learning. Participants will explore advanced topics such as NLP models, transformer architectures, vision transformers, LLM frameworks, and research trends in Generative AI and LLMs. The sessions will be led by eminent faculty from academia and industry, ensuring a rich learning experience. Daily expert talks will provide practical insights and the latest advancements in the field.
